SOP-001 · Client Onboarding
New client onboarding process
Owner: HR & Ops Tool: ClickUp + Slack Review cycle: Quarterly
Purpose
To ensure every new client is onboarded consistently, with full visibility across the team, clear task ownership, and no steps missed. Replaces ad hoc onboarding that previously caused delays and duplication.
Process steps
1
Create client folder in ClickUp
Set up a new client space using the standard onboarding template. Assign team members to relevant tasks. Set due dates for each onboarding milestone.
ClickUp · Onboarding template
2
Send welcome pack and intro Slack message
Use the standard welcome message template in Slack #client-onboarding channel. Tag the assigned account lead. Confirm client has received login credentials and access.
Slack · #client-onboarding
3
Complete intake form and add to CRM
Record all client details in the intake form. Update GoHighLevel CRM with contact info, service tier, and account lead. Flag any special requirements in the client notes field.
GoHighLevel CRM
4
Schedule kickoff call and update ClickUp
Book kickoff call within 3 business days of contract sign. Update ClickUp task status to "Kickoff scheduled." Notify team in Slack #ops-updates.
ClickUp · Slack · #ops-updates
5
Mark onboarding complete and archive
Once all onboarding tasks are ticked off, mark the ClickUp project as complete. Move to "Active clients" folder. Post completion update in Slack #client-onboarding.
ClickUp · Slack
Time saved
Reduced onboarding time by eliminating ad hoc back-and-forth
Key outcome
Consistent client experience from day one, every time
Adopted by
Whole company — used for all new client onboarding
SOP-002 · HR Operations
New hire onboarding & offboarding process
Owner: HR & Ops Tool: ClickUp + Slack Review cycle: Bi-annual
Purpose
To standardise the onboarding and offboarding of all Flow Candy team members, ensuring no steps are missed, access is correctly provisioned and removed, and every new hire has a consistent first-week experience.
Onboarding steps
1
Create ClickUp onboarding checklist
Duplicate the standard new hire checklist template. Assign to HR lead and hiring manager. Set Day 1, Week 1, and Month 1 milestones.
ClickUp · New hire template
2
Provision system access
Grant access to ClickUp, Slack, GoHighLevel, and any role-specific tools before Day 1. Confirm access with new hire via Slack DM on morning of first day.
ClickUp · Slack · GoHighLevel
3
Introduce in Slack #team channel
Post a welcome message in #team using the standard intro template. Include role, start date, and who they report to. Ask team to respond with a welcome.
Slack · #team
4
Complete HR documentation
Ensure signed contract, emergency contacts, and bank details are received within first 3 days. File in HR folder. Update team roster in ClickUp.
ClickUp · HR folder
Offboarding steps
1
Notify team and update ClickUp
Update ClickUp team roster. Reassign all open tasks to relevant team members. Notify in #ops-updates with last working day.
ClickUp · Slack · #ops-updates
2
Revoke all system access on last day
Remove access to ClickUp, Slack, GoHighLevel, and all other tools on last working day. Confirm completion in ClickUp offboarding checklist.
ClickUp · All systems
3
Conduct exit documentation
Complete exit paperwork. Archive HR records. Document any handover notes in ClickUp for the incoming team member or line manager.
ClickUp · HR folder
SOP-003 · Reporting & Data
Weekly operations reporting process
Owner: HR & Ops Tool: ClickUp + Slack Cadence: Weekly (every Monday)
Purpose
To ensure leadership has a consistent, accurate view of operational performance every week — covering task completion rates, workload distribution, and any blockers — without requiring manual data chasing across the team.
Process steps
1
Pull weekly ClickUp report (Monday 9am)
Export task completion data from ClickUp for the previous week. Check overdue tasks, completion rates by team member, and workload distribution.
ClickUp · Reports dashboard
2
Update weekly ops tracker
Enter key metrics into the weekly ops Excel tracker: tasks completed, tasks overdue, new tasks created, and any flagged blockers from the team.
Excel · Weekly ops tracker
3
Post summary in Slack #ops-updates
Share a brief weekly summary using the standard format: tasks completed, tasks overdue, any blockers, and key priorities for the week ahead. Tag relevant team leads.
Slack · #ops-updates
4
Escalate blockers to leadership
Any tasks overdue by 3+ days or flagged as blocked must be escalated to the relevant manager via Slack DM or ClickUp comment within 24hrs of the report.
Slack · ClickUp · @manager
5
Archive report and reset tracker
Save completed weekly report to the ops archive folder. Reset the weekly tracker for the new week. Confirm archive complete in ClickUp.
ClickUp · Archive folder
Key outcome
Leadership has full operational visibility every Monday without chasing the team

75% manual workload reduction through systematic process design
