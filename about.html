About me
Hi. I'm Hafsa.

I'm good at coming into a situation and figuring out what it needs. Not in a grand way. Just practically. Someone needs a system built. A process documented. A project that's been running on good intentions and spreadsheets finally given some structure. That's usually where I come in.

I've done this in a lot of different places. A mental health non-profit I founded at 19. A community programme for 150 girls in Karachi. A London charity with 80 years of donor data and no way to make sense of it. A children's safeguarding team at a local council. Each one different. Same underlying thing I was doing in all of them.

I moved to London a couple of years ago. It was hard at first, and also exactly what I wanted. I'm still building here. Still figuring things out. Currently I'm spending a lot of time thinking about where AI and automation fit into operational work, because I think that intersection is genuinely interesting and most organisations are only just starting to take it seriously.

If you want the formal version of all this, the CV and project work are elsewhere on this site. This page is just me.

The journey so far
2019
Founded Waqfiyaat
A mental health non-profit, started from nothing while studying full time. 100+ clients over 4 years. My first real experience of building something people actually relied on.
2022
Pak Alliance for Math and Science
Project Lead for a programme that got 150 girls into exam preparation across 6 government schools in Karachi. Also helped write the first minimum education standards for the province of Sindh. Secured $10,000+ in grant funding.
2023
Moved to London. Joined Flow Candy.
New country, new sector. Built automation systems and HR workflows for a startup. Started properly learning what operations looks like when you build it right rather than just keeping up with it.
2024
Medical Research Foundation
Went through 160+ legacy giving records going back 80+ years and made sense of them. Built the dashboards. Contributed to improving the processes and documentation the team ran on.
2025
London Borough of Barking and Dagenham
Supporting children's safeguarding teams. High volume, high stakes, not a lot of room for error. Building the systems and processes that hold it all together.
Now
Figuring out AI and automation
Using AI tools every day. Building automations. Exploring what it looks like when ops work gets smarter. This bit is still in progress and I'm genuinely enjoying it.

Families in Karachi, senior leadership in London, frontline social workers, startup founders. I adjust how I communicate. I don't adjust what I bring.
